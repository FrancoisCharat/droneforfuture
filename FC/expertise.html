Areas of Expertise

Specialized knowledge across multiple domains

Systems Thinking

Systems thinking is a holistic approach to analysis that focuses on how a system's constituent parts interrelate and how systems work over time and within the context of larger systems.

My expertise includes causal loop diagramming, system dynamics, soft systems methodology, and critical systems thinking applied to organizational design, policy analysis, and complex problem solving.

Key strengths include identifying leverage points for intervention, mapping complex relationships, anticipating unintended consequences, and designing sustainable solutions.

Design Systems

Design systems are comprehensive frameworks that standardize and streamline the design and development process, ensuring consistency, efficiency, and scalability across products and platforms.

My approach includes atomic design methodology, component-driven development, design tokens, and accessibility integration, with a focus on both technical implementation and organizational adoption.

Benefits include improved design consistency, accelerated development cycles, enhanced collaboration, and reduced technical debt.

Digital Twins

Digital twins are virtual replicas of physical assets, processes, or systems that serve as real-time digital counterparts, enabling monitoring, analysis, simulation, and optimization.

My implementation expertise includes 3D modeling, IoT sensor integration, real-time data processing, simulation engines, and machine learning for predictive capabilities.

Applications include critical infrastructure monitoring, emergency response training, predictive maintenance, and healthcare facility optimization.
