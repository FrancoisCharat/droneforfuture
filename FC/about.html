About Me

Systems thinking specialist with expertise in complex systems implementation

Background

With an Industrial PhD in Complex Systems, Artificial Intelligence, and Robotics from Paris-Saclay University and Stanford University, I bring a unique perspective to complex systems challenges.

As a certified ultralight and remote pilot, I founded DFF (Drone for Future) in 2018, incubated at Airbus BizLab/IMT, developing innovative drone-based solutions for critical infrastructure.

My work spans multiple industries, from healthcare and emergency services to smart buildings and education, always applying systems thinking principles to create effective solutions.
